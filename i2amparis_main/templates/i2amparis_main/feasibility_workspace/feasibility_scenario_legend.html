Baseline: Current policies implemented until 2030 and extending the equivalent carbon price in each region, growing at the rate of GDP per capita from 2030 onwards
CP_EI: Current policies implemented until 2030 and keeping emissions intensity of GDP reduction rate same as 2020-2030 period after 2030
NDC_EI: Unconditional Nationally Determined Contributions implemented on top of current policies until 2030 and keeping emissions intensity of GDP reduction rate same as 2020-2030 period after 2030
